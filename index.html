Recycle Soccer

Collecting and distributing donated soccer equipment to underserved children.

We believe that all children deserve an equal opportunity to participate in a sport that the world collectively loves, and that participation starts with access to quality equipment.

Accepted Donations

We collect new and used soccer balls, jerseys, cleats, and equipment.

Recipients

We distribute the items locally to Dallas-Fort Worth area children and internationally through mission trips.

Origin

On a 2019 trip to Costa Rica, Elliott and Finley gifted a duffle bag of soccer balls to the children of Guapiles. Their faces beamed with gratitude and excitement and immediately began to play. For many, it was their first ball. Soccer is the world's most popular sport, but quality equipment is inaccessible to millions. We hope to change that!

Donations or Requests?

Please email us if you would like to donate soccer equipment or if you aware of kids in the Dallas-Fort Worth area that are in need of these items.
